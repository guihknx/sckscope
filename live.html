uScope!

MUTE YOUR SPEAKERS or USE HEADPHONES! | Works best in a well lit room. | Allow camera access or try: sckScope
This toy will put live video into a kaleidoscope and move to the sound of whatever input you give it. You can either use a microphone or give it a feed from an mp3 player, guitar or from your mixer (Hint-Hint, dj's and vj's). Please mute your computer's output as this is still in beta. Works best in Chrome. Some features and controls are not available in Firefox.

-Josh
